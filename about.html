About Us

Parivar Care is a digital platform dedicated to improving elderly care by simplifying medication management, enhancing doctor-patient communication, and keeping families informed. Created by Yash S from IIT Madras, we provide a seamless way for elderly patients, their doctors, and their children to stay connected, ensuring accurate medication tracking and easy access to healthcare.

Our mission is to make elderly care more efficient, compassionate, and family-oriented, with the vision of empowering families to manage their loved ones' health with confidence.

Tagline: "Bringing Care Closer to Home."
